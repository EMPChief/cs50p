Goal:
    Calculate energy using Einstein’s formula E = m × c².

Input:
    - Mass (integer)
      Example: 1, 14, 50

Output:
    - Energy (integer)
      Format: "E: <value>"

Process / Steps:
    1. Ask user for mass:
        - Prompt: "m: "
    2. Receive input and convert to integer
    3. Define constant:
        - c = 300,000,000 (speed of light)
    4. Calculate energy:
        - E = m × c²
    5. Display result:
        - Print "E: <energy>"

Example:
    Input: 1
    Output: E: 90000000000000000
